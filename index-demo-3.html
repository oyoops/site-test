Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our ATM Raid Bot and services.

What are the objectives of this Agency?

Our objective is to provide the best raidbot on Telegram for Solana communities, enabling them to reward raiders with their native token.

What is the best feature of the ATM Raid Bot?

Our AI grades raid replies based on their quality and rewards users accordingly, ensuring only the best contributions get rewarded.

How does the AI grading system work?

The AI evaluates the raid replies based on predefined criteria, which can be customized by project admins to better align with their goals.

How can I start using the ATM Raid Bot?

To get started, simply contact us via our website to set up the ATM Raid Bot for your community.
